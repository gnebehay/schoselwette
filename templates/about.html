How it works

  • Participation fee: 11â‚¬ / 12CHF.
  • Collect points by correctly guessing match results.
  • The collected money of all participants will be re-distributed to the top three participants at the ratio 60:30:10.
  • Different from previous instances of the Schoselwette, this time it is only possible to bet on a three-way outcome.
    • - Team 1 wins, Draw, Team 2 wins (in short: 1, X, 2) - due to a highly novel and sophisticated system for awarding points.
  • Bets are made for the final outcome of the game before a potential penalty shootout.
  • Bets can be changed until the beginning of the match.
  • New and important! Points are awarded the following way: For each match, the odds for individual match results (1, X, 2) are computed according to this formula:

    odds = number_of_bets / bets_for_result

    For example, if there are 10 bets for a match, 7 of which are for team 1, 2 of which are for a draw and 1 is for team 2, then the odds will be:

    Odds for team 1: 10/7 = 1.42
    Odds for a draw: 10/2 = 5
    Odds for team2: 10/1 = 10

    If you guessed the result of a match correctly, you will get the odds of the result awarded as points. The reasoning behind this new system is to award a much larger amount of points to bets that have a rather unlikely outcome.
  • The odds for a match will be displayed as soon as the match begins.
  • In the group stage, you can mark 4 matches as super bets. These matches will yield double points.
  • Guessing the champion correctly will yield 10 additional points.
  • The matches of the knockout phase will be added as soon as the participating teams are known.
